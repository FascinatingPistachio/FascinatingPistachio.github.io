Team Gelatofish

A team of people who use Discord, making it sweeter for everyone.

Join Discord (Coming Soon)

Our Mission

Team Gelatofish is a team of passionate Discord users. We believe customization shouldn't be locked behind a paywall, so we foster community creativity by building tools that allow users to express themselves freely.

What We Offer

  • Animated profile pictures & dynamic avatars
  • Custom, non-official badges for engagement
  • Free client-side decorations & banners

About The Project

Team Gelatofish is dedicated to bringing \"Free Nitro\" features to everyone through client modding. We centralize tools for animated avatars, banners, and decorations in one convenient place.

Why We Vet Requests

  • Prevent Copying: We respect intellectual property and do not allow copying official Discord assets to avoid legal issues.
  • Ensure Safety: We screen for inappropriate content to keep the ecosystem clean and safe for all users.

Our goal is to make downloading and applying custom PFPs, banners, and decorations completely hassle-free and open source for the community.
