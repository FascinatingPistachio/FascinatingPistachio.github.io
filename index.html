About Me

Hey there! My name’s Aaron, and welcome to my little corner of the web. I’ve always loved trains — something about them just fascinates me, especially the old steam ones. They’ve got this amazing power and character that you just don’t see anymore. One of my main hobbies (and kind of a goal, honestly) is to get a picture with as many steam trains as possible. Every time I visit a heritage railway or museum, I’m always on the lookout for another one to add to my collection!

When I’m not out spotting trains, I really enjoy painting miniatures. There’s something super satisfying about taking a bunch of little plastic parts, using pliers to snip them off the sprue, gluing them together, and turning them into a finished model. Then, of course, painting them is the best part — watching them come to life with color. I’ve been into Warhammer 40,000 models lately, and it’s just a fun, relaxing hobby for me.
