About Me

Hello there! My name is Aaron, and welcome to my website. I love steam trains. Something about them has always fascinated me. One of my main hobbies (and kind of a goal) is to get a picture with as many of them as I can. Whether it’s at a heritage railway, a museum, or even on the main line, if I see one that’s the real deal (replicas count if they’re full scale), I try to get a photo.

I sometimes paint miniatures, but I mostly enjoy playing video games like Fortnite, Roblox, Minecraft, or other games on my Nintendo Switch 2.
