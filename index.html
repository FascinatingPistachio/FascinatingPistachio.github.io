About Me

Hello and welcome! My name is Aaron. This website is a place for me to share some of the things I'm passionate about.

I just really like trains in general, especially the historic steam locomotives you can find at railway museums and heritage lines. There's something truly special about seeing these incredible machines up close. The gallery here is a passion project – I'm trying to collect as many photos as possible of *me* next to these amazing trains! You'll see my collection so far in the gallery.

Beyond trains, I have a few other hobbies. I enjoy painting miniatures, particularly Warhammer 40,000 models. I also like playing video games on my Nintendo Switch 2 and spending time with my pet dog.

I also enjoy watching anime, which got me interested in learning a bit of Japanese (still very much a beginner!). Some of the series I'd recommend are:
