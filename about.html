My Hobbies & Interests

Steam Trains: My fascination with steam locomotion started young. I love the mechanical complexity and the history behind these machines. The National Railway Museum is basically my second home!

Miniature Painting: When I'm not gaming or train-spotting, I enjoy painting miniatures. It requires patience and a steady hand, but seeing a grey plastic model turn into a colorful character is very rewarding.

Gaming: I'm a big Nintendo fan. Currently rotating between Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom and Mario Kart 8 Deluxe. I also play a lot of Roblox with friends.
